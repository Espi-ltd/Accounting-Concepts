Online Tax and Refund Info

Check Your Refund Status

Visit www.irs.gov/refunds to check the status of your federal refund. It’s quick, easy and secure. Have your SSN or ITIN available, your filing status and your exact refund amount ready. Be sure to wait four weeks after your paper return has been mailed.

View Your Tax Information

Visit the Michigan Siebel eService site to view your Michigan refund status and income tax information. To log in have your SSN and last name ready.
